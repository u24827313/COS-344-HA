W - Move Camera Up
A - Move Camera Left
S - Move Camera Down
D - Move Camera Right

T - Move Camera Up Slowly
F - Move Camera Down Slowly

+ - Zoom In
- - Zoom Out

Q - Rotate Camera CW [Scene Moves CCW]
E - Rotate Camera CCW [Scene Moves CW]

R - Reset
Esc - Mouse Movement [Broke]

L - Switch Shader
